# Install/Setup
- Download via git or manual download options (Also available in the HumHub marketplace!).
- Sign in/up to [BuyMeACoffee](https://www.buymeacoffee.com/) then navigate to [Dashboard - Widgets](https://www.buymeacoffee.com/dashboard) page.
- Copy your `username` then navigate to your HumHub instance's Admin Panel > BMC Settings and place your `username` then save.
